Music Lovers Club at Cornell

Our mission:

The purpose of the Music Lovers Club at Cornell is to foster a vibrant community through the shared love of music, offering events and activities that explore diverse genres and deepen musical knowledge. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment wherein members can discover new music, celebrate their favorites, and engage with the broader musical community.

Our goals:

The goals of this organization are to build a diverse, inclusive, and engaging community through music, to encourage friendly discourse on individual opinions on different genres of music, and to create a space wherein members can learn more about different music and cultures.
